# Guide: InsightEd Guide for School Heads

**Objective:** To equip School Heads with the strict technical protocols required for 100% Data Health compliance, secure account management, and reliable offline data synchronization for accurate DepEd reporting.

> **Note:** This guide is strictly intended for the **Phase II of the InsightED Pilot Test**.

---

## Module 1: Pre-requisite - App Installation
**Objective:** Since InsightEd is a Progressive Web App (PWA) it operates directly from your mobile browser rather than App Store/Play Market. This ensures offline-readiness on all modern smartphones.

1. **Open your Browser:** Open Google Chrome (Android) or Safari (iOS/iPhone).
2. **Navigate to the App Link:** Type the exact web address into your URL bar: `tinyurl.com/InsightEdV2`
3. **Wait for Load:** Allow the login screen to fully render.
4. **Initiate Installation:**
   - **For Android (Chrome):** Tap the **[Install App]** button on the bottom of the screen (or the three vertical dots menu at the top right, then select "Install App" or "Add to Home screen").
   - **For iOS (Safari):** Tap the **[Share]** icon (the square with an arrow pointing up at the bottom of the screen), scroll down, and tap **[Add to Home Screen]**.
5. **Confirm and Launch:** Confirm the installation prompt. Return to your phone's home screen and launch the app via the new **InsightEd** icon.

## Module 2: Registration & Account Creation
**Objective:** Establish your secure, official School Head account strictly tied to your institution's School ID.

1. **Access Registration:** From the login screen, tap the **[Create Account]** link at the bottom.
2. **Select Role:** Under "Registering As", tap the dropdown and select **[School Head]**. A 5-step hierarchical School Selection menu will securely load.
3. **Establish School Linkage:**
   - Select your **Region**.
   - Select your **Division**.
   - Select your **District**.
   - Select your **Municipality**.
   - Select your specific **School**.
4. **Verify Username:** The system will automatically generate your Username, which is your 6-digit **School ID**. Keep this safe.
5. **Enter Contact & Security Details:**
   - **Official DepEd Email:** Enter your official DepEd email. The system forces the domain to be `@deped.gov.ph` for rigorous compliance. (Example: `juan.delacruz`).
   - **Mobile Number:** Enter an active 11-digit mobile number starting with `09` (e.g., `09123456789`).
   - **Password:** Enter a strong password and repeat it in the **Confirm Password** field.
6. **Confirm Location:** Scroll down to the Geo-Tagging Map. Drag the pin to adjust the exact location of your school if necessary.
7. **Submit:** Tap the **[Register Account]** button.
8. **Save IERN:** Upon successful registration, a pop-up modal will display your **InsightEd ID (IERN)**. Screenshot or write this unique Control Number down immediately. Tap **[Got it, I'll Save it]**.

## Module 3: Administrative Sign-In
**Objective:** Securely access the InsightEd platform.

1. **Launch the App:** Open the **InsightEd** app from your home screen.
2. **Access the Login Screen:** Locate the **Email or School ID** input field.
3. **Select Official Credentials:** Enter your 6-digit **School ID** as your Username. (Although you registered a `@deped.gov.ph` email for communication, the system strictly assigns your uniquely generated School ID as your primary login credential for security mapping). Enter your **Password**.
4. **Sign In:** Tap the **[Sign In]** button.
5. **Grant App Permissions:** Upon first login, the app will prompt for device access. You must tap **[ALLOW]** for **Camera**, **Location**, and **Storage**.
> **Warning:** These permissions are absolutely non-negotiable. Location and Camera access are strictly required for the **Geo-Tagging** feature and uploading verifiable photographic evidence for compliance.

## Module 4: Achieving the "100% Data Health" Mandate
**Objective:** Fulfill all institutional reporting requirements to achieve a flawless Data Health Score.

### Step-by-Step Data Entry:
1. **Access School Forms:** From the Home Dashboard, tap the **[Forms]** icon on the bottom navigation bar.
2. **Select the Module:** Tap on a specific form card, such as **[School Profile]**.
3. **Complete Mandatory Fields (Example: School Profile):**
   - **School ID (6-Digit):** Enter your ID and tap the **[Validate]** button adjacent to it.
   - **School Name:** Enter the WHOLE NAME without abbreviations (e.g., "National High School").
   - **Curricular Offering:** Tap the dropdown and select your offering (e.g., "All Offering (K-12)").
   - **Is this an Annex School?:** Tap **[Yes]** or **[No]**. If Yes, provide the **Mother School ID**.
   - **Geo-Tagging:** Scroll to the map section and drag the pin to set your school's exact latitude and longitude.
4. **Adhere to Validation Rules:** Ensure numerical fields (like student tallies) only contain numbers. Exceeding character limits or entering invalid formats will highlight the field in red and prevent submission.
5. **Attach Evidence-Based Reporting:** For qualitative assessments (e.g., in Physical Facilities), tap **[UPLOAD PHOTO]** to attach clear, recent photographs using your built-in camera. A 100% Data Health Score strictly requires these visual proofs.
6. **Save Changes:** Tap the **[Save Profile]** or **[Save Changes]** button at the bottom of the screen.
7. **Confirm Acknowledgment:** Check the boxes confirming you are the **School Head** and that the information is true, then tap **[Confirm]**.
8. **Conduct Final Review:** Navigate to the **[Review Summary]** tab.
> **Pro-Tip:** Do not submit until you verify that every module displays a solid green checkmark (‚úÖ). Incomplete sections will display a warning icon (‚ö†Ô∏è) and drastically reduce your Data Health rating.

### Key Form Requirements:
Ensure you correctly input the following critical fields per form to achieve 100% completion:

**1. School Profile & Information**
- **Personal Details:** Provide your Name, Position, and Date Hired. Auto-fill functionality is available using your Plantilla Item Number via CSV lookup.
- **Geo-Tagging:** Manually drag the map pin to record precise latitude and longitude coordinates.

**2. Enrollment & Organized Classes**
- **Enrollment:** Enter accurate learner counts per grade level based on your curricular offering. Do not forget to complete the **ARAL program** data.
- **Organized Classes:** Input the precise number of sections for each grade level, adhering to DepEd class size standards.

**3. Learner Statistics**
- **Specialized Categories:** Use the Grid section to record detailed headcounts across categories: *SNEd, Disability, ALS, Muslim, IP, Displaced, Repetition, Overage, and Dropout* per grade level.

**4. Shifting & Modalities**
- **Scheduling:** Specify the shifting schedule per grade level (*Single, Double, or Triple shift*).
- **Delivery Modes:** Select the standard learning delivery mode (*In-Person, Blended, Distance Learning*). Explicitly declare emergency Alternative Delivery Modes (ADMs) if utilized.

**5. Teaching Personnel & Specialization**
- **Headcounts & Experience:** Under **Teaching Personnel**, provide total teacher headcount per grade level (ES, JHS, SHS) and for Multigrade classes. Breakdown teachers by their experience profile.
- **Individual Records:** Under **Teacher Specialization**, use the **[+ Add Teacher]** button to input specific teachers. Provide their *Name*, *Position*, *Specialization*, and configure their exact daily teaching load in *Hours* and *Minutes*.

**6. School Resources**
- **Site & Utilities:** Map your institution's **Buildable Space** explicitly by dropping map pins to calculate area in square meters. Declare your Electricity and Water Sources.
- **Facilities & Equipment:** Categorize toilets by male, female, or common/shared. Ensure you count **TOILET SEATS/BOWLS**, not rooms/doors. Declare functional and non-functional counts for Laptops, TVs, Printers, Desks, and Armchairs.
- **e-Cart Batches:** Record procured batches by tapping **[+ Add Batch]**. Specify *Batch No.*, *Source of Fund*, number of laptops, and whether it *Includes Smart TV*.

**7. Physical Facilities**
- **Building Inventory:** Classify buildings under **[+ Add Newly Built Building]** or **[+ Add Good Condition Building]**. Input *Building Name*, *Category* (e.g., Gabaldon, QRF, Repairs), *Storeys*, and *Classrooms*.
- **Repair Assessment:** Tap **[+ Add]** to register a building, then tap **[+ Add Room Assessment]**. Select items needing repair (e.g., *Roofing, Wall, Flooring*) and attach a *Recommended Action* (e.g., Routine Repair, Major Repair / Rehabilitation).
- **Demolition Records:** Identify buildings for demolition under **[+ Add Building for Demolition]** and select strict justification criteria (*Age/Dilapidation, Safety Hazard, Calamity Damage, or Site Upgrade/Repurposing*).

## Module 5: Understanding Your Data Health Score
**Objective:** Comprehend the automated scoring algorithm and apply strategies to ensure an 'Excellent' 100% or a 'Good' 90% rating.

The InsightEd platform features an advanced Data Health and Fraud Detection engine that computationally evaluates your submissions every night. Every school starts with a base score of **100 points**. 

### How Points Are Deducted:
1. **Critical Missing Data (-25 points per issue):**
   - Reporting enrollment without reporting corresponding teachers, classrooms, or toilets.
   - Forgetting to breakdown teacher experience or class section details.
2. **Data Consistency Mismatches (-25 points per issue):**
   - Headcounts don't align (e.g., Total teaching personnel vs. specialized teachers, or student enrollment vs. organized class sections).
   - Unrealistic ratios (e.g., an average class size exceeding 65 students or falling below 15 students).
3. **Statistical Outliers (-10 to -30 points):**
   - The system flags abnormal ratios against regional standards (e.g., Pupil-to-Teacher, Pupil-to-Classroom, Pupil-to-Toilet, Pupil-to-Furniture, Pupil-to-Seat). 
4. **Multivariate Inconsistencies (-20 points):**
   - The algorithm detects highly unusual combinations of enrollment and resources that contradict typical school profiles.

### Scoring Tiers & Validation:
- **100 (Excellent):** Perfect submission. No issues detected.
- **90-99 (Good):** Minor statistical outliers.
- **50-89 (Fair):** Significant missing data or multiple inconsistencies.
- **<50 (Critical):** Immediate attention required.

### Strategies for a Perfect 100% Score:
1. **Never Leave Zeros on Critical Resources:** If your school has enrolled learners, zero counts for teachers, classrooms, or toilets will trigger massive penalties. 
2. **Match Your Sub-Totals:** Always double-check that your detailed breakdowns (like teacher experience or grade-level sections) sum up exactly to the total counts provided.
3. **Declare Temporary Facilities:** If your enrollment is high but classrooms are low, ensure you declare temporary learning spaces or shifting modalities to mathematically justify the high Pupil-to-Classroom Ratio.
4. **Complete the Specialization Form:** Ensure that at least 50% of your total reported teachers are registered with a defined specialization and teaching load.

## Module 6: Offline Guide & Data Synchronization
**Objective:** Safeguard your data during field inspections with limited internet connectivity.

1. **Work Offline Confidently:** The InsightEd app utilizes a local-first architecture. If you lose internet connection while filling out forms, continue working. A banner stating **"üìµ Offline - Read Only"** may appear for some live validations, but your typed inputs are securely saved to your local cache.
2. **Monitor Pending Forms:** Forms completed without an internet connection are safely held in the app's internal memory queue.
3. **Execute the Sync Protocol:** Once you return to an area with a stable Wi-Fi or cellular data connection, navigate to the **[Sync]** dashboard via the bottom navigation menu.
4. **Initiate Transfer:** Check the list of pending items. Tap the **[Manual Sync]** or **[Sync All]** button to transmit your encrypted local data to the central database. Wait until you see a success confirmation notification on the screen.
> **CRITICAL WARNING:** Never log out of your account, clear the app's internal storage/cache, or uninstall the InsightEd app while you have forms pending sync. Doing so will result in the permanent, irrecoverable loss of that data.

## Module 7: Maintenance & Troubleshooting
**Objective:** Maintain optimal application performance and resolve common glitches without risking data loss.

1. **Resolve UI Glitches:** If the app freezes, form templates appear blank, or drop-down menus fail to load, navigate to your **[Settings]** or **[Profile]** menu using the bottom navigation bar.
2. **Clear Cache/Troubleshoot:** Tap the **[Troubleshoot]** or **[Clear Cache]** button. This safely refreshes the underlying form schemas and clears temporary memory without deleting your local drafts or pending sync data.
3. **Ensure Protocol Compliance:** Every Monday morning, navigate to the same **[Settings]** menu.
4. **Check for Updates:** Tap **[Check for Updates]**.
> **Pro-Tip:** Performing this step weekly guarantees your app is equipped with the latest DepEd-mandated reporting templates, form options, bug fixes, and vital security patches for the week ahead.

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

**1. I forgot my password, what do I do?**
On the Login screen, tap the **[Forgot Password]** or **[Troubleshoot]** link. Enter your 6-digit School ID to request a secure password reset link, which will be sent to your registered official DepEd email address.

**2. I forgot my username, what do I do?**
Your username is strictly your 6-digit **School ID** only. Do not enter your personal DepEd email (e.g., `juan.delacruz@deped.gov.ph`) or your school's official email address. Enter the School ID and your password to sign in successfully.

**3. What is the secure code when registering as a School Head?**
School Heads **do not** require a secure Authorization Code to register. The secure code is an internal control mechanism strictly reserved for Regional Offices, Division Engineers, and Central Office personnel.

**4. My app is not saving the data I submitted, what do I do?**
This is typically caused by running an outdated, out-of-sync version of the application. On your dashboard or login screen, click **[Troubleshoot]** and select **[Check for Updates]** to ensure your app is running the latest release.

**5. Who can I contact for technical support?**
You may contact **support.stride@deped.gov.ph** via Google Chat for quick technical support.
